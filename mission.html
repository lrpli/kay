Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

The values and purpose that guide everything we do at Kaysville Elementary PTO.

Our Mission

To foster a welcoming and inclusive community that empowers parents, teachers, and staff to work together in support of every student's academic success, personal growth, and well-being at Kaysville Elementary School.

Our Vision

A School Where Every Child Belongs

We envision Kaysville Elementary as a place where every child feels safe, valued, and excited to learn — and where every family feels genuinely welcomed and heard.

We believe the best schools are built on strong partnerships between home and classroom. When parents are engaged, teachers are supported, and the community rallies around its kids, remarkable things happen.

Our vision is simple: a school community so connected, so caring, and so committed to its students that every child has the best possible start in life.

🏫

Building Community

Every event, every fundraiser, every volunteer hour is a building block in the stronger community our kids deserve.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

These six values shape every decision we make and every program we run.

🤝

Partnership

We believe parents and teachers are stronger together. Collaboration between home and school is at the heart of everything we do.

❤️

Inclusion

Every family, every student, every background is welcome. We actively work to ensure no one feels left out or left behind.

⭐

Excellence

We hold high expectations — for our students, for our volunteers, and for the quality of experiences we create together.

🌱

Growth

Learning doesn't stop in the classroom. We support the whole child — their curiosity, character, and confidence.

🎉

Joy

School should be a joyful place. We bring fun, celebration, and warmth to every event we organize.

🏛️

Integrity

We operate transparently, spend responsibly, and honor the trust families place in us with every dollar donated.
