Our Programs & Events

Year-round initiatives that enrich the learning environment and celebrate our school community.

All Programs

How We Make a Difference

From classroom grants to community carnivals, every program is designed to put students first and strengthen the bonds between families and teachers.

Annual Event

Fun Run & Fundraiser

Our biggest event of the year! Students collect pledges and complete laps around the school track. The Fun Run combines healthy activity with the PTO's most important fundraising effort.

500+ students participate each year
Learn More
Year-Round

Classroom Grants Program

Teachers apply for mini-grants to purchase books, supplies, and materials that go beyond the standard budget. Every dollar helps turn classroom ideas into real learning experiences.

30+ teachers supported annually
Learn More
Annual Event

Teacher Appreciation Week

One full week dedicated to honoring the incredible educators and staff at Kaysville Elementary. Volunteers coordinate meals, gifts, and heartfelt notes from students and families.

35+ staff members celebrated
Learn More
Year-Round

Reading Incentive Program

Students log reading minutes throughout the year to earn rewards and recognition. The program encourages a love of reading at home and in the classroom.

1,000+ books read each school year
Learn More
Enrichment

Arts & Music Enrichment

The PTO funds assemblies, performances, and arts experiences that inspire creativity. From in-school concerts to visiting artists, we believe arts education matters for every child.

All students participate each semester
Learn More
Support

Field Trip Assistance

We partner with teachers to help fund field trips and ensure that every student can participate regardless of their family's financial situation. No child misses out.

No student is ever left behind
Learn More
Seasonal Event

Fall Harvest Festival

A beloved fall tradition where families gather for games, crafts, costumes, and community fun. The Harvest Festival kicks off the school year with excitement and connection.

Hundreds of families attend
Learn More
Recognition

Student of the Month

Each month, teachers nominate students who exemplify Kaysville Elementary's core values. Honorees are recognized at assemblies and celebrated with their families and peers.

10 students honored every month
Learn More

Every Dollar Goes Back to Our Kids

The Kaysville Elementary PTO is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All funds raised through our programs and events are reinvested directly into programs that benefit Kaysville students and staff. Donations are tax-deductible.
