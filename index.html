What We Do

Supporting Students Every Day

Our PTO works year-round to provide resources, events, and community connections that make Kaysville Elementary a great place to learn and grow.

📚

Classroom Support

We fund classroom supplies, books, and learning tools so every teacher has what they need — and every student can thrive.

🎉

School Events

From the Annual Fun Run to Family Game Night, we create memorable experiences that bring the whole community together.

🏫

Teacher Appreciation

Our educators go above and beyond — we celebrate them every week and especially during Teacher Appreciation Week.

🌟

Student Recognition

We spotlight student achievements in academics, the arts, and character — because every child deserves to feel seen.
