Our Story

History of the PTO

From humble beginnings to a thriving school community — the story of Kaysville Elementary PTO.

How It Started

Built by Parents, for Students

The Kaysville Elementary PTO was born from a simple idea: that parents and teachers working side by side could make a meaningful difference in the life of every student. A small group of dedicated families at Kaysville Elementary came together with a shared belief — their school deserved more support, more celebration, and more connection.

What started as informal gatherings to discuss classroom needs quickly grew into an organized, official Parent-Teacher Organization. Those early volunteers set the tone that still defines us today: approachable, committed, and always putting students first.

Today, the Kaysville Elementary PTO is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving hundreds of students and families in the heart of Kaysville, Utah. We are proud of how far we've come — and excited about where we're headed.

501(c)(3)

Registered Nonprofit

The Kaysville Elementary PTO is a fully registered 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization, making all eligible donations tax-deductible. EIN: 99-3677288.


Every dollar we raise goes back into programs, events, and resources that directly benefit the students and staff of Kaysville Elementary.
